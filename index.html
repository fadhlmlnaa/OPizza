Neapolitan

Origins (18th Century) : Neapolitan pizza emerged as a popular food for the working class in Naples. Initially sold by street vendors, it was a simple flatbread topped with garlic, lard, and salt, evolving over time with the addition of tomatoes brought from the Americas

Quattro Stagioni

"Quattro Stagioni" (Four Seasons) pizza is a classic Italian pizza that features four distinct sections, each representing one of the four seasons of the year. Each quarter of the pizza is topped with different ingredients, symbolizing a different season.
